Announcements

A happy late new year to all of you! To celebrate the new year, I’m refreshing my site with a new coat of paint. My old site was a bit too hard to manage, since it was created on top of a highly-customized Hugo theme that did not really have extensibility as a selling point. I’ve now build a new site on top of the beautiful, minimal, and highly-customizable Eureka theme, and taken this time to re-imagine how I will use the site.

I will try to keep my homepage up-to-date with my research output, thanks to a specific widget focused on this. Announcements, updates, and general academic material will exist here in the Posts section, while mixing pot of other topics will stay in the Gumbo section.
